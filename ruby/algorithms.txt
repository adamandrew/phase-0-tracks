Elevator Robot
- When a human(s) enter the elevator:
	- Ask each human which floor to go to.
		- Press the close button.
		- Press the floor button that the human said.
		- When the floor is reached:
			- Open and hold the door open until human leaves the elevator or wait for 10 seconds
		- Repeat until there is no human left in elevator

Cooking
- Check if there is enough ingredient
	- If there is:
		- Get all ingredients out.
		- Chop if needed.
		- Put into pot to cook.
		- Cook for 1 hour and check.
			- If food is done:
				- Serve and eat
			- If food is not done:
				- Cook for 10 more minutes and check again
	- If there is not enough:
		- Go do grocery.
